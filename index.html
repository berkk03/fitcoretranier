Privacy Policy

FitCore Trainer respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

FitCore Trainer may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Appointment and scheduling data
  • Client management data created by trainers or clinics

This information is used only for managing appointments, clients, and training sessions within the application.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Manage appointments between trainers and clients
  • Track session credits or training packages
  • Provide scheduling and management features
  • Improve application performance and functionality

Data Storage

Application data may be stored securely using cloud services such as Firebase or other secure backend systems.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except when required to operate the application services.

Third-Party Services

The application may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase (for authentication and database)
  • Cloud hosting services

These services may process limited data required for application functionality.

User Data Control

Users may request deletion of their data by contacting us using the contact information below.

Children's Privacy

FitCore Trainer is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
